Social activities

In January 2014, as the publicity ambassador of RCY(Korean Red Cross Youth), I attended the CEO commendation ceremony and bread making service activities, and conveyed the message of hope. In February, the group served as the publicity ambassador of Jiangnan District with SHINee, hoping to attract more foreign tourists to visit The district. In December 2015, the group donated part of the proceeds from their second winter special album Sing For You to SMile For U, a charity organization, to support the music education of Children in Asia. In the same month, we sent charcoal balls to poor families together with CHEN KAI. In September 2016, our group EXO became the brand image spokesperson of Skecker Asia Pacific. Our team members will carry out promotional activities in various parts of Asia Pacific successively
